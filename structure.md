Drone Inspection for Smarter Construction Projects 
At Terrabyte Global Ltd, we are transforming construction monitoring and inspection through 
advanced drone technology. Our drone inspection services provide accurate, real-time aerial 
insights that help construction teams build smarter, faster, and safer, from pre-construction 
planning to project completion, our drones deliver high resolution imagery and 3D models. 

Our Drone Construction Solutions 
• Progress Monitoring 
Track construction milestones with detailed aerial images and videos that clearly show 
progress over time. 
• Structural Inspection 
Inspect roofs, facades, towers, bridges, and hard-to-reach structures without scaffolding or 
manual climbing. 
• Site Mapping & 3D Modeling 
Generate accurate orthomosaic maps, digital surface models (DSM), and 3D models for 
planning and analysis. 
• Safety & Compliance Inspections 
Identify potential hazards, ensure regulatory compliance, and enhance worker safety through 
frequent aerial checks. 
Revolutionize Your Farm with Aerial Intelligence 
At Terrabyte Global Ltd, we are redefining agricultural monitoring and management. Our 
advanced drone solutions provide precise, real-time aerial insights, empowering farmers to 
optimize crop health, maximize yields, and manage resources efficiently for smarter, more 
sustainable farming.

Our Agricultural Drone Solutions: 
1. Crop Health Monitoring 
• Multispectral & Hyperspectral Imaging: Detect plant stress, disease, or nutrient 
deficiencies early by analyzing light reflected from crops. 
• NDVI Mapping: Assess crop vigor and identify areas that require attention using 
vegetation indices. 
2. Precision Agriculture 
• Variable Rate Application: Guide targeted spraying of fertilizers, pesticides, or 
herbicides to specific areas, reducing waste and costs while improving efficiency. 
3. Farm Mapping & Surveying 
• Topographic Mapping: Generate accurate 2D maps and 3D models of your farm for 
effective planning and analysis. 
• Contour & Slope Analysis: Support erosion control, drainage planning, and land 
preparation for better crop management. 
4. Farm Asset Inspection 
• Monitor and assess farm infrastructure, equipment, and resources using drones to 
ensure optimal performance and operational efficiency. 
Telecommunications  
At Terrabyte Global Ltd, we deliver advanced drone solutions that support the planning, 
inspection, and maintenance of telecommunications infrastructure. Our aerial intelligence 
provides accurate, real-time insights that help telecom operators improve network reliability, 
reduce downtime, enhance safety, and optimize operational efficiency. 
Our Telecommunications Drone Solutions 
1. Tower & Mast Inspection 
• Structural Integrity Assessment: Inspect towers, masts, antennas, and mounting 
systems for defects, corrosion, misalignment, or damage—without climbing or service 
interruption. 
• High-Resolution Visual & Thermal Imaging: Identify loose components, 
overheating equipment, and potential failure points early. 
2. Network Planning & Site Surveying 
• Aerial Site Surveys: Capture precise data for new tower installations, upgrades, and 
network expansion projects. 
• Terrain & Obstruction Analysis: Evaluate line-of-sight, terrain elevation, and 
surrounding obstacles to optimize signal coverage and placement. 
3. Asset Monitoring & Maintenance 
• Routine Asset Monitoring: Track the condition of telecom assets over time to 
support proactive maintenance and lifecycle management. 
• Post-Installation Verification: Confirm correct installation and compliance with 
design specifications. 
4. Emergency & Damage Assessment 
• Rapid Response Inspections: Quickly assess network damage after storms, 
accidents, or natural disasters to accelerate restoration efforts. 
• Minimized Downtime: Enable faster decision-making and targeted repairs through 
real-time aerial data

## About US

At Terrabyte Global Limited, we provide comprehensive geospatial, surveying, and spatial 
intelligence solutions that empower informed decision-making across infrastructure, 
environmental, and technology-driven sectors. By combining advanced geospatial 
technologies, industry-standard methodologies, and a team of skilled professionals, we 
deliver accurate, reliable, and actionable spatial insights. 
Our drone surveillance and inspection services deploy cutting-edge unmanned aerial Vehicle 
(UAV) for site monitoring, infrastructure assessment, security surveillance, and operational 
analysis.  
We specialize in geospatial data acquisition using both aerial and ground-based platforms,

Drone Survey, Mapping, Surveillance & Inspection 
We deliver high-precision drone surveying, mapping, surveillance, and inspection using 
advanced unmanned aerial systems. Our services support construction monitoring, asset

Geographic Information System (GIS) 
Our GIS solutions turn spatial data into actionable intelligence through mapping, 
visualization, spatial analysis, and decision-support systems. We support governments and
